Remembering Steve.


Over a million people from all over the world have shared their memories, thougths, and feelings about Steve. One thing they all have in common — from personal friends to colleagues to owners of Apple products — is how they've been touched by his passion and creativity. You can view some of these messages below. And share your own at rememberingsteve@apple.com



Steve Jobs


What impact has Steve Jobs had on my life? Every short story I've written as an adult (and certainly everyone that has been published), almost every email I've ever written on machines created by Apple. I interacted online using an Apple machine long before I ever encountered a PC. Steve Jobs helped to realize the Star Trek future I dreamed of as a kid. And through his vision, I have been able to realize my own. You'll be missed, Mr. Jobs. Thanks for all your amazing work
